Vision

"An Educated and enlightened society of GNH, built and sustained on unique Bhutanese values of THA - DAM-TSIG & LEY-GJU- DREY"


Mission

  • Develop sound educational policies that enable the creation of a knowledge-based GNH society.
  • Provide equitable, inclusive and quality education and lifelong learning opportunities to all children and harness their full potential to become productive citizens.
  • Equip all children with appropriate knowledge, skills and values to cope with the challenges of the 21st century.

Objectives

  • To prepares citizens to become knowledgeable, skilful, creative, innovative, enterprising, and capable of responding to the national needs and emerging global trend.
  • To develop citizens that value Bhutan’s unique national identity, ancient wisdom and culture, prepared for right livelihood, and practice contemplative learning.
  • To develop individuals who are lifelong learners, who have a holistic understanding of the world and a genuine care for others and nature.
  • Develop citizens’ competency to deal effectively with the contemporary world, who are critical, creative, informed and engaged in civic affairs.
  • To enable effective and efficient ICT Service delivery.
